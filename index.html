Instant Noodle

Description

A mass of dried precooked noodles fused with oil, usually eaten after being soaked in boiling water for 3 to 5 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Noodle packet
  • Eggs
  • Coriander

Steps

  1. Boil the water for about one minute
  2. After it started boiling, add the packet of noodle
  3. While it's cooking, boil an egg on another pot
  4. Add seasoning to the noodle
  5. Take out the egg and peel
  6. Cut the egg into two half
  7. After the noodle is cooked, turn off the stove
  8. Put the cooked noodle into the bowl and egg on the top
  9. Garnish it with little bit of coriander

Mango Pudding

Description

This simple mango dessert recipe is so easy to make. The pudding is extremely smooth and tasty.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup hot water
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 packags powdered gelarin
  • 26 ounces canned mango pulp
  • 1 ½ cups evaporated milk
  • 1 cup cold water

Steps

  1. Combine hot water, sugar, and powdered gelatin in a small bowl; stir until sugar and gelatin are dissolved.
  2. Mix mango pulp, evaporated milk, and cold water together in a large bowl. Pour in hot water mixture and stir well.
  3. Refrigerate until set, at least 4 hours.

Simple Dumpling

Description

As a kid I begged my mom to make chicken and dumplings all the time. Now I swear by this simple recipe too!

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • ¾ teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons shortening
  • ¾ cup milk

Steps

  1. Whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt in a mixing bowl. Cut in the shortening with a knife or pastry blender until the mixture resembles fine crumbs. Stir the milk into the flour mixture until moistened. Dough will be pretty lumpy, but be sure to not over mix it!
  2. Drop dough by large spoonfuls onto any boiling stew. The dumplings should rest on top of vegetables and meat; it's okay if they stick out of the top a bit. Cook uncovered for 10 minutes; cover and cook another 10 minutes.
